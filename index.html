About

I am a Master's student in Mechanical Engineering at Columbia University and a Research Collaborator at the Leon Root, MD Motion Analysis Lab at Hospital for Special Surgery. My research focuses on developing low-cost, computer vision-based methods for rigid-body 3D kinematics, with applications in ultrasound bone reconstruction and human motion analysis. I specialize in the biomechanics of the shoulder complex, working on projects that introduce novel marker-based protocols for humeral and scapular tracking in both clinical and research settings.
